# Stem-cell-derived islets (SC-islets)

## Current role in this corpus

SC-islets appear here as the pancreas branch's core screening platform: a stem-cell-derived endocrine baseline that only becomes convincing after functional maturation is demonstrated.

## Working synthesis

- In this corpus, SC-islets are not treated as screen-ready just because beta-cell markers are present.
- Hogrebe 2021 supplies the production-friendly baseline, while Balboa 2022 supplies the functional, metabolic, and cytoarchitectural maturity checks that make downstream screening claims believable.
- This entity therefore marks a pancreas-specific rule: first build a reproducible SC-islet baseline, then prove secretion-linked maturity, and only then trust a perturbation or screening branch.

## Main tension

- scalable endocrine production versus incomplete functional maturity
- marker-positive identity versus secretion-credible screening readiness
