# Nephron-patterning kidney organoids

## Current role in this corpus

Nephron-patterning kidney organoids appear here as the main hPSC kidney-platform branch for choosing among ureteric-bud, nephron-progenitor, broad renal, or segment-biased differentiation routes.

## Working synthesis

- In this corpus, kidney organoid work is less about whether renal tissue can be made at all and more about which developmental route or segment balance should anchor the platform.
- Xia 2014 anchors a ureteric-bud-oriented route, Takasato 2016 anchors a broader renal-compartment build, Morizane 2016 sharpens nephron-progenitor control, and Vanslambrouck 2023 pushes segment-biased proximal-tubule refinement.
- This entity therefore sits before later support or assay layers such as flow, perfusion, or monocyte rescue: first pick the kidney-patterning route that matches the disease, readout, or segment question.

## Main tension

- broader renal complexity versus tighter progenitor control
- generic kidney identity versus segment-enriched optimization
