## Working synthesis

- In this collection, PDO is usually the first translationally useful cancer or donor-state baseline rather than a late-stage add-on.
- The main payoff is practical: donor state, expandable culture, and clinically interpretable downstream perturbation can coexist in one platform.
- PDO therefore sits one step before immune coculture, engineering, or PDO-X escalation. It is the branch used to decide whether a project even deserves those extra layers.

## Main tension

- donor preservation and translational relevance versus missing stromal, immune, or vascular context
- expandable practical baseline versus incomplete tissue complexity
