# Blood-brain-barrier (BBB) spheroids

## Current role in this corpus

BBB spheroids appear here as barrier-first assay systems for penetration and exclusion triage, rather than as developmental brain organoids.

## Working synthesis

- In this collection, BBB spheroids are useful when the question is not generic neural development or infection, but whether a cargo crosses a selective barrier at all.
- Their main advantage is that barrier selectivity is the primary design objective from the start: endothelial, pericyte, and astrocyte contact plus penetration readout are built together.
- They therefore belong beside organ-on-chip and other access-control platforms, but are distinct from them: BBB spheroids are barrier-triage-first rather than compartment-control-first.

## Main tension

- direct barrier triage and throughput versus incomplete flow, immune traffic, and wider neurovascular realism
- assay focus versus reduced flexibility compared with broader neural or chip systems
