# Assembloids and regional fusion

## Current role in this corpus

Assembloids and regional fusion appear here as the main modular strategy for moving from single-region brain organoids to inter-compartment circuit or signaling questions.

## Working synthesis

- In this collection, assembloids are the clearest answer when a single organoid no longer captures the relevant interregional projection, modulation, or compartment-specific interaction.
- Sloan 2018 supplies the foundational fusion logic, Miura 2022 turns that logic into an assay-ready circuit workflow, and Human 2026 extends it into neuromodulatory disease questions.
- This entity therefore sits between baseline regional patterning and host-engraftment claims: first decide whether modular fusion inside the dish can answer the question before escalating to transplantation.

## Main tension

- modular control over interacting compartments versus extra assembly burden and fusion variability
- clearer interregional logic versus loss of fully endogenous co-development
