## Notion Summary

**한 줄 요약:** TransCODE/PeptideAtlas/HUPO-HPP/HIPP/GENCODE가 7,264 human non-canonical ORF를 proteomics + HLA immunopeptidomics + Ribo-seq + 진화보존 + CRISPR으로 재분류. 핵심 개념 'peptidein' — translation 확인되었으나 정상 생리 기능 미입증 산물.

**과제 관련성:**

Tumor immunopeptidome 항원 후보 공간이 conventional coding mutation 넘어섬. HLA immunopeptidomics에서 1,785/7,264 ncORF 검출 — cryptic peptide와 peptidein이 암 항원 발굴 source. ncORF/peptidein layer를 한미암 antigen pipeline에 추가, normal physiology vs tumor-restricted antigen 구분.

**주요 결과:**

- Conventional proteomics 484 peptides, 183/7,264 ncORFs.
- HLA immunopeptidomics 3,116 peptides, 1,785/7,264 ncORFs.
- HLA-linked ncORF Ribo-seq manual validation 613/691 (88.7%).
- HLA binding prediction concordance 94.8%.
- Initial peptidein annotations 121, pan-essential KO signature 51.
- c10riboseqorf92: OLMALINC/LINC00263 123-aa peptidein, 415/485 cancer model essential.
