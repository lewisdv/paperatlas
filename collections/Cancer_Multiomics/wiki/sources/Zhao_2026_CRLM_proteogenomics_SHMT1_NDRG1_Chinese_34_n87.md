## Notion Summary

**한 줄 요약:** FUSCC Shanghai treatment-naive CRLM 34명 matched T+N+LM 102 sample에 WES+RNA-seq+TMT proteome+Ti4+-IMAC phospho + independent IHC validation n=87. SHMT1-formate-AMPK 축과 PIM 기반 NDRG1 Ser330p-ubiquitin degradation 축 in vivo 검증. LM proteome unsupervised C1(metabolism, poor) / C2(RNA function, better).

**과제 관련성:**

한국인 mCRC 코호트 설계 시 matched T+N+LM 트리오 + 4-layer 동일 protocol reference. SHMT1 inhibitor(SHIN1/SHIN2)·AMPK agonist LM subset 평가 경로. NDRG1 S330p subset → TP-3654(pan-PIM) 적용 후보. FTCD+GPD1+SOD2+EIF4B S422 4-marker IHC panel 한국인 코호트 외부 재현 후보. iProx IPX0007391001 + GSA PRJCA020890 공개 데이터 cross-reference.

**주요 결과:**

- 34명 treatment-naive CRLM = 102 samples (matched T+N+LM, FUSCC 2012-2019).
- WES 19,493 nonsyn; TMT proteome 8,093; phospho 16,300 class I; IHC validation n=87.
- SHMT1 high → OS+PFS poor; SHMT1 KD → ↓growth, formate rescue, AICAR mitigation in vivo.
- NDRG1 S330A → ↓liver metastasis; TP-3654 3μM ↓NDRG1 S330p, ↓migration/invasion, ↑p-AMPK.
- LM proteome C1 vs C2 OS log-rank p<0.05; KEGG C1 metabolism, C2 RNA function.
- 4-marker IHC FTCD+GPD1+SOD2+EIF4B S422 validation n=87 OS/PFS p<0.05.
- CPTAC GBM/LUAD/CCRCC/PDAC에서 SOD2/GPD1/EIF4B S422 외부 재현.
