## Notion Summary

**한 줄 요약:** 중국 Harbin treatment-naive HER2-low BC 83 + HER2-high 32명에 WES+RNA-seq+global proteome+phospho+lactylome(18,214 sites/1,644 proteins) 적용. 3 단백체 subtype(PS1 endocrine / PS2 anti-angiogenic / PS3 32.53% anti-HER2). IHC 10-단백질 SVM AUROC 0.80.

**과제 관련성:**

PS3(32.53%) ↔ DESTINY-Breast04 T-DXd ORR ~40% 정합 — 한국인 HER2-low T-DXd 후보 stratification. IHC 10-단백질 deployable classifier(AUROC 0.80)는 proteome 없는 임상 사이트에서도 PS subtype 분류 가능. Lactylome 신규 modality 도입 cost-benefit 평가 첫 reference. PRKDC K2694/K2908 lactylation → DNA-PK 의존 치료저항 가설.

**주요 결과:**

- 115 tumor + 135 NAT + 14 RWD + 17 PDO (Harbin).
- WES 204×T / 120×N. Proteome 8,307 / phospho 43,963 sites / lactylome 18,214 sites.
- PS3 32.53% → trastuzumab/T-DM1 후보.
- SVM-RFE 10 feature classifier train AUROC 0.97 / test 1.00; IHC 10단백 AUROC 0.80.
- PDO: PS1 ↑ endocrine, PS2 ↑ anti-angiogenic, PS3 ↑ anti-HER2.
- RWD n=14 anti-angiogenic: PS2 3/4 CR/PR vs PS1+PS3 1/10.
- PRKDC + STAT1 + AURKB lactylation 신규.
- 7q gain, 9p loss + MTAP cis-deletion → HER2-low 한정 poor prognosis.
