# ETV2-driven vascular induction

## Current role in this corpus

ETV2-driven vascular induction appears here as the canonical engineered route for planting vascular-like support inside an otherwise non-vascular host organoid, especially in the brain branch.

## Working synthesis

- In this corpus, ETV2 is the clearest transcription-factor shortcut for generating endothelial-like support without leaving the dish.
- Cakir 2019 uses it to rescue hypoxia and apoptosis inside cortical organoids, while L 2026 shows how orthogonal activation can push the same logic toward dedicated vessel-only builds.
- This entity therefore marks an engineering move rather than a whole biological endpoint: use it when integrated in-dish vascular support matters more than preserving a fully unguided developmental path.

## Main tension

- rapid programmable vascular support versus added genetic-engineering burden
- integrated in-dish rescue versus concerns about engineering-driven distortion of native development
