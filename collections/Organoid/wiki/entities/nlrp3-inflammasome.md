# NLRP3 inflammasome

## Current role in this corpus

NLRP3 appears in this collection as an inducible disease-assay system rather than as a baseline organoid-building target.

## Strong supporting pages

## Working synthesis

- The cerebral branch uses NLRP3 to turn mature organoids into slice-based neuroinflammation assays with astrocyte-weighted readouts.
- The cardiac branch uses inflammatory activation to test whether a macrophage-integrated heart assembloid can be pushed into atrial-fibrillation-like electrical instability.
- Together these papers make NLRP3 a cross-organ example of the same design move: start from a mature multicellular organoid, then add a tightly defined inflammatory trigger and readout stack.

## Main tension

- mechanistic inflammatory assay clarity versus the missing immune compartments of simplified organoid systems
- acute inducible phenotype versus broader chronic disease interpretation
